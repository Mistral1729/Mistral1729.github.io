Free pure maths resources

Last Updated - 2023.06.15

Some free resources for content roughly equivalent to that of an undergraduate degree in pure maths, with some graduate courses for good measure.

Core Courses (first two years roughly)

  1. Introduction to Proofs

    2. Reading: Richard Hammack Book of Proof
    Lectures: Michael Penn Proof Writing

  2. Number Theory

    3. Reading: George Andrews Number Theory
    Lectures: Introduction to number theory (Berkeley Math 115)

  3. Combinatorics

    4. Reading: Richard Brualdi Introductory Combinatorics
    Lectures: Combinatorics (IIT Madras)

  4. Real Analysis

    5. Reading: Basic Analysis: Introduction to Real Analysis
    Lectures: MIT 18.100A Real Analysis, Fall 2020

  5. Linear Algebra

    6. Reading: Linear Algebra Done Right
    Lectures: Michael Penn Abstract Linear Algebra

  6. Abstract Algebra

    7. Reading: Knapp Basic Algebra
    Lectures: Michael Penn Abstract Algebra

  7. Complex Analysis

    8. Reading: Ahlfors Complex Analysis
    Lectures: Richard Borcherds Complex Analysis Lectures

  8. General Topology

    9. Reading: MAT327 Topology Notes
    Lectures: MTH 427/527 Introduction to General Topology

  9. Measure Theory

    10. Reading: Measure, Integration and Real Analysis
    Lectures: IMPA Masters Program: Measure Theory (2018)

Elective Courses (for the remaining year/years)

  1. Mathematical Logic

    2. Reading: Open Logic Project
    Lectures: Introduction to Mathematical Logic (Berkeley Math 125)

  2. Topics in Combinatorics

    3. Reading: Gowers Techniques in Combinatorics Lecture Notes
    Lectures: Topics in Combinatorics (Cambridge Part III course)

  3. Additive Combinatorics

    4. Reading: Gowers Introduction to Additive Combinatorics Lecture Notes
    Lectures: Introduction to Additive Combinatorics (Cambridge Part III course)

  4. Extremal and Probabilistic Combinatorics

    5. Reading: Extremal and Probabilistic Combinatorics
    Lectures: IMPA Masters Program: Extremal and Probabilistic Combinatorics (2017)

  5. Graph Theory and Additive Combinatorics

    6. Reading: Yufei Zhao Graph Theory and Additive Combinatorics Lecture Notes
    Lectures: MIT 18.217 Graph Theory and Additive Combinatorics, Fall 2019

  6. Analytic Number Theory

    7. Reading: Introduction to Analytic Number Theory Lecture Notes, Math 531
    Lectures: 2022 Spring Analytic Number Theory

  7. Probability Theory

    8. Reading: Varadhan Probability Theory (Courant Lecture Notes in Mathematics)
    Lectures: Probability Theory I (UCSD Math 280A), Probability Theory II (UCSD Math 280B), Probability Theory III (UCSD Math 280C)

  8. Ergodic Theory

    9. Reading: Ergodic Theory With A View Towards Number Theory
    Lectures: Ergodic Theory, Geometry and Dynamics (Harvard Math 275)

  9. Statistical Physics

    10. Reading: Statistical Mechanics of Lattice Systems
    Lectures: Introduction to Statistical Mechanics (2019)

  10. Functional Analysis

    11. Reading: Brezis Functional Analysis, Sobolev Spaces and PDEs
    Lectures: MIT 18.102 Introduction to Functional Analysis, Spring 2021

  11. Commutative Algebra

    12. Reading: Eisenbud Commutative Algebra With A View Toward Algebraic Geometry
    Lectures: Commutative Algebra (Berkeley Math 250B)

  12. Algebraic Geometry

    13. Reading: Gathmann Class Notes Algebraic Geometry, Vakil Foundations Of Algebraic Geometry Notes
    Lectures: Algebraic Geometry 1 : Varieties (Berkeley Math 256A)

  13. Differential Geometry

    14. Reading: Reyer Sjamaar Manifolds and Differential Forms
    Lectures: ICTP Claudio Arezzo Differential Geometry

  14. Algebraic Topology

    15. Reading: Hatcher Algebraic Topology
    Lectures: Pierre Albin Algebraic Topology
